Who we are

The landscape of healthcare delivery is rapidly changing, and telehealth and remote patient monitoring are here to stay.

Remote patient care is still in its infacy, but many stakeholders are seeing great value of this new form of healthcare delivery. In the midst of rapid paradigm shifts, we asked ourselves one bold question: What if remote patient care were a primary means of healthcare delivery? How would we design a technology solution to support such a new model? How should we capture the full value of remote care to fairly compensate both providers and payers?

We are experts in building innovative health technologies, navigating various government programs, and analyzing complex healthcare data. We have built and operated several successful healthcare companies and medical groups in the past. We founded Orma Health to push the envelope of remote patient care.

What we do

We closely work with innovative and like-minded providers and payers. We will work as a technology partner for building and rolling out your remote patient care business. With our technology solutions, our partners can capture the full value of remote patient care.

Risk Adjustment

Our patent-pending solution identifies/captures accurate risk factors using remote patient monitoring devices and telehealth technologies.

HEDIS and Star Ratings

Quality gaps are identified in real-time and addressed using our innovative techology.

Preventing Hospitalization

Clinical rules for real-time alerts and warnings detect/prevent any signs for hospitalization events.

Fee-for-Service

Remote Patient Monitoring CPT codes are automatically captured to get reimbursed for the Medicare Fee-for-Service patients.
